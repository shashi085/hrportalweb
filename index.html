How HR Portal works

Three steps. No training sessions. No new apps to learn.

Step 1

Ask anything

"How many leave days do I have?" "What's the maternity policy?" "Apply 3 days leave next week." Employees use plain language — no forms required.

Step 2

Understands your company

HR Portal reads your company's policy documents and answers from your actual policies — not generic internet knowledge. Every answer is cited and traceable.

Step 3

Takes action

It doesn't just answer — it acts. Submit leave, retrieve payslips, check balances, route approvals. Managers review team requests in the same interface.

Multi-tenant Ready

Built for companies with multiple teams, departments, or entities. Each tenant is fully isolated with its own data and policies.

Role-based Access

Employees see only their own data. Managers see only their team. HR admins have full visibility. Access is enforced at every layer.

Built with data responsibility in mind

HR Portal is designed with data minimisation from the ground up. Employee data is scoped by role and company. We do not use your company's data to train AI models. Early access customers work directly with our team on deployment and data handling.
