How HR Portal works

Three steps. No training sessions. No new apps to learn.

Step 1

Ask anything

"How many leave days do I have?" "What's the maternity policy?" "Apply 3 days leave next week." Employees use plain language — no forms required.

Step 2

Understands your company

HR Portal reads your company's policy documents and answers from your actual policies — not generic internet knowledge. Every answer is cited and traceable.

Step 3

Takes action

It doesn't just answer — it acts. Submit leave, retrieve payslips, check balances, check your employment status, or route confirmation approvals. Managers and HR complete workflows in the same interface their employees use.

What makes HR Portal different

HR Portal is not another menu-heavy HR system. It is a chat-first layer for answers, actions, and approvals.

Ask in plain language

Employees ask questions or request HR actions the same way they would message HR — without searching menus, forms, or shared folders.

Grounded in your company policies

Answers come from your actual HR documents, so employees get guidance based on company policy instead of generic AI responses.

Actions, not just answers

HR Portal can trigger controlled workflows such as checking status, submitting requests, routing approvals, or updating records through secure backend tools.

One experience for every role

Employees get self-service, managers handle team actions, and HR keeps oversight — all within the same role-aware interface.

Team context built in

Employees can understand their manager, peers, and reporting chain, while HR maintains the official organization structure.

Permission-aware by design

Every answer and action is scoped by role, tenant, and permission, so people only see and do what they are allowed to.

Built with data responsibility in mind

HR Portal is designed with data minimisation from the ground up. Employee data is scoped by role and company. We do not use your company's data to train AI models. Early access customers work directly with our team on deployment and data handling.

✓ Role-scoped data — employees only see what they should
✓ Your data is never used to train AI models
✓ Fully isolated per company — no data cross-contamination
